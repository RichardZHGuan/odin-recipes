Mapo Tofun Noodles

Description

Delicious mapo tofun (hidden noodle style). After creating the tofun you will be able to get all the ladies.

Ingredients

  • DOUBANJIANG
  • Green onions
  • Soy Sauce
  • minced chicken breast
  • tofu from foodymart
  • shaoxing wine
  • garlic
  1. throw in chicken breast with garlic to wok or pan
  2. stir fry until breast cooked
  3. add in sauces (to taste) and tofu
  4. let simmer and stir fry a bit more after
  5. garnish with green onions and it's lit
  6. pour tofun over bed of noodles and serve
